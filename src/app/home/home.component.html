FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about our luxury garments

What makes your garments unique?
+

Each piece is meticulously handcrafted using heritage techniques passed down through generations. We source only the finest materials from prestigious mills worldwide and maintain the highest standards of craftsmanship.

How do I care for my luxury garments?
+

We recommend professional dry cleaning for most pieces. Each garment comes with detailed care instructions. For cashmere and delicate fabrics, use specialist cleaning services to maintain their beauty and longevity.

What is your sizing guide?
+

Our garments are cut to classic European sizing. We provide detailed size charts for each category. For personalized fit advice, our customer service team is available to assist with measurements and recommendations.

Do you offer international shipping?
+

Yes, we ship worldwide with complimentary shipping on orders over £200. International orders typically arrive within 5-7 business days and include full customs handling.

What is your return policy?
+

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn items in original condition. Returns are complimentary with prepaid return labels. Custom and personalized items are final sale.

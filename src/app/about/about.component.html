Our Story

Founded in Paris in 2018, Luxette began as a vision to create sustainable, elegant fashion that empowers women while respecting our planet. We believe that fashion should be both beautiful and responsible.

Our journey started with a simple question: How can we create clothing that makes women feel confident and beautiful while minimizing our environmental impact? The answer led us to develop partnerships with ethical manufacturers and source only the finest sustainable materials.

Our Values

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental footprint through sustainable materials, ethical manufacturing, and responsible packaging.

Quality

Every piece is crafted with attention to detail and made to last, ensuring you can enjoy our designs for years to come.

Empowerment

We believe fashion should empower women to feel confident, comfortable, and authentic in their own skin.

Innovation

We continuously explore new sustainable technologies and design approaches to push the boundaries of eco-friendly fashion.
